About Us

Looking to stem the rampant cancer epidemic, The Cancer Documentary addresses the worldwide cancer epidemic head-on, exploring an aspect of cancer that is often overlooked, its connection with diet and lifestyle. Through the eyes of cancer patients and survivors, doctors and experts in the field, the film seeks to shine light on the importance of living a healthful lifestyle. Learn how making simple changes can contribute to a cancer-free life and help our bodies fight optimally, if we do have cancer.
